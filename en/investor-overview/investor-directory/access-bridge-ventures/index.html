How we do it

Access Bridge Ventures is a leading early stage venture capital fund. Investing in entrepreneurs across the Middle East, North Africa, Pakistan and on a case by case basis outside these regions.

Follow us on LinkedIn, Twitter

 

 

We look for exceptional leaders with an aim to support their journey as early as the first institutional investor. When we invest, we like to lead the round and support our partners throughout their journey.

 

We look to provide value add through leveraging our exceptional networks across the region, our collective investment and operational experiences, strong governance and overall discipline.
