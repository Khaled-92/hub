Who we are
Investing in leading growth-stage companies in emerging markets

Global Ventures backs global-minded founders that are leading growth-stage companies and using technology to transform emerging markets and the world.

The venture capital firm invests in emerging-market founders on a mission to change the world.

Focusing on enterprise technology investments

Through its team of driven, passionate and curious individuals, Global Ventures operates in an ever-changing and dynamic marketplace.

The company creates bridges between industry veterans and disruptive innovators, between the west and the east. Global Ventures mindfully allocates capital and leverages expertise to catalyze growth and create the next generation of success stories.

 

Driven by curioristy and a global perspective

Global Ventures is a regional venture capital fund backing global-minded founders, leading growth-stage companies and using technology to transform emerging markets, and the world.
