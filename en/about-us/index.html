How we do it

We are global tech ecosystem in the heart of Abu Dhabi, enabling startups to grow. 

As part of the government’s efforts to diversity the economy, Hub71 is a key driver of the Ghadan 21 program and the catalyst to Abu Dhabi’s digital transformation.

Set against a backdrop of state-of-the-art infrastructure through our strategic partner ADGM, we provide founders access to top investors, world-class customers, expert mentorship and lucrative market opportunities.

We provide tech startups programs, services and equity-free incentives to unlock new opportunities for their global growth.
