Precious Payload builds an API-SaaS software to help space entrepreneurs test tech and business ideas 10x faster and 5x cheaper

Precious Payload is creating an easy-to-use tool that will enable faster decision-making in space projects. We’re packing it with enterprise software for simple, safe, and fast launch booking, as well as space mission supply chain management. Our goal is to help space entrepreneurs test business and technology hypotheses ten times faster and five times cheaper.

 

 

 

Providing secure cross-border digital tools to manage the supply chain in space industry

‘Time-to-next-milestone’ is the space mission metric we’re after. We’re engaging the aerospace community to unify the flow of information and simplify space project supply chains: launch booking, insurance procurement, ground services, etc. We analyze the data and build it into secure cross-border software.

 

 

Precious Payload is creating an easy-to-use tool that will enable faster decision-making in space projects
