We are the #1 Arabic Edutainment Provider for Kids

We offer children access to content that not only supports the educational curriculum, but also teaches life lessons and helps them to discover their talents.

We have developed an extensive range of interactive content that both teaches and entertains children by giving them the social, critical thinking, and educational skills they need to support their growth.

Great Talent + R&D = Success

R&D is an integral but expensive part of any EdTech venture. The initiatives of Hub71, ADEK, Ghadan 21, and ADRDA, to create a world-class R&D ecosystem allowed us to attract top talent from the Emirate and abroad. 

“We wanted to combine the passion and ambitions of Abu Dhabi with the best practices that have made Helsinki one of the top global places in terms of educational rankings.”
