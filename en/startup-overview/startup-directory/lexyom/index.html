Who we are
Offering the best legal advice in the Middle East through online legal consultations and legal services.

Lexyom provides experienced, international, and price predictable legal services combining cutting edge legal knowledge with state of the art technology.

The platform gathered the best lawyers and law firms in the Arab world, including Lebanon and the United Arab Emirates to offer the best legal advice in the Middle East through online legal consultations and legal services.

 

Providing legal advice and expertise digitally

Lexyom has combined the best of technology with expert legal knowledge to provide a seamless legal experience.

Once users submit a legal consultation request, Lexycon will get more familiarized with the request through a video call from an experienced lawyer and will follow-up in case of any missed information. The platform helps users create and launch groundbreaking products, services, concepts and close deals and to protect your interests and stand out.

 

 

 

The first online legal platform of its kind crafted by super lawyers, geeky developers and excellent executers.

Lexyom has earned a reputation for crafting innovative business and financial solutions and developing precedent-setting legal strategies to achieve their clients' goals.

A team committed to providing quality legal services without any compromise on standards and professionalism, Lexyom is the go to for legal and is trusted by hundreds of customers and lawyers in their daily interactions. It is secure, easy to use and affordable for people seeking justice.
