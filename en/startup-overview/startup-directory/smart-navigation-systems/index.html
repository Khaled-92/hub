Who we are
Smart Navigation Systems help clients develop prototypes in the early stages of examining and testing their aimed final products.

Smart Navigation Systems is an outdoor to indoor location-based solution that provides wayfinding and location-aware messaging.

The company provides first-class, quick, secure, sustainable and cost effective location based services.

 

 

Efficient | Cost Effective | Sustainable

Smart Navigation Systems help clients in defining their requirements, designing systems which best fit their needs, implementing quality and quick systems and ensuring sustainable operation and maintenance services. All of this is delivered at reasonable costs ensuring return on investment for clients.

 

Developing state-of-the-art indoor location systems.

Smart Navigation Systems provide first-class, quick, secure, sustainable and cost effective location based services. The company achieves this utilizing their long-term experience and partnership with the best technology providers.

The company is contributing to the UAE Government’s initiative to build a knowledge-based society and economy in cooperation with local authorities.
