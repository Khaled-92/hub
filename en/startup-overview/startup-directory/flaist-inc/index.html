Who we are
Helping Banks and Financial Institutions enhance the digital experience of their customers

Flaist is on a mission to democratize the digital transformation process for Banks and Financial Institutions through technology.

The startup helps companies build amazing customer experiences using its handcrafted technology stack components with a goal to deliver beautiful customer journeys.

 

Contemporary AI-powered digital experience platform built for Banks and Financial Institutions

Flaist empowers customers with conversational intelligence across mediums such as Voicebot, Chatbot, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Siri, Google Assistant, Website, Samsung Bixby, enabling natural language-based conversational bots that understands languages and dialects.

The company also delivers contextual and friendly nudges to your users for various use cases using in-app notifications or push notifications, and generates advanced analytics to drive actionable insights based on emerging patterns and trends.

 

A team of seasoned entrepreneurs with the mission to democratize digital transformation for enterprises

Flaist brings together technologists, bankers, healthcare professionals, data scientists and security experts who have built enterprise products for multiple startups and multi-billion dollar organizations like Citibank, Mastercard, HPE, ArcSight, and Oracle.

It is built by a team of seasoned entrepreneurs with the mission to democratize digital transformation for enterprises.
