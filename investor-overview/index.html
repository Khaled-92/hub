Invest in a Better Tomorrow

There is certainly more than one type of investor here at Hub71, and we provide everything you need to capitalize on growth opportunities in one of the world’s fastest-growing economies.

Growth investors

Angel investors are an important part of our capital community, making an immediate impact on the early stages of a startup’s growth. As attracting angel investment is a logical step for our community of founders, we are easing ways for angel investors to form legal investment structures and reap the benefits of being based in Abu Dhabi’s thriving tech ecosystem.

At Hub71, our seed stage investors facilitate access to capital though our leading regional and global venture capital funds and investors that are deepening the pool of capital available in Abu Dhabi. We provide a gateway to an award-winning International Financial Centre through our partner, Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM), which also delivers a progressive business environment to ease funding opportunities into Hub71’s tech startups.

We live in an age of limitless potential. Ever since our formation, Hub71’s top priority has been to fulfil the growth potential of our burgeoning community of startups, each looking to disrupt their industries. It’s fundamental to what we do and underpins our effort to cultivate a community of growth investors that can capitalize on the abundance of transformative ideas and growth opportunities before the world knows it.
