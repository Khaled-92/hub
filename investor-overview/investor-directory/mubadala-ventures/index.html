Who we are
A global ventures platform

Based in the world’s leading technology hubs, and with an international network, the Mubadala Capital Ventures platform is uniquely positioned to support the founders building tomorrow’s leading companies.

 

 

Our Investment Approach

The Ventures team’s approach is deeply rooted in partnering with entrepreneurs and investors and takes a long-term perspective on building leading technology companies. It supports outstanding founders through deep domain expertise and active involvement, providing access to Mubadala’s extensive assets globally, and financially supporting its winning companies through their growth life cycle.

 

Mubadala Capital Ventures are always open to outstanding founders applying technology in novel ways and building defensible business models.

Core investment themes include:
Enterprise and the cloud, Logistics and mobility, Tech-enabled healthcare, Fintech and insuretech, Consumer and marketplaces.
