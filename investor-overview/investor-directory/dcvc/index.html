Who we are
Investing in deep tech to make real societal impact

DCVC backs entrepreneurs solving trillion-dollar problems to multiply the benefits of capitalism for everyone while reducing its costs.

For over twenty years, DCVC and its principals have backed entrepreneurs applying Deep Tech, from the earliest stage and beyond, to pragmatically and cost-effectively tackle previously unsolvable problems in nearly every industry, especially those that haven’t seen material technological progress in decades.

 

DCVC is a Seed and Series A Deep Tech venture fund

DCVC brings to bear a unique model that unites a team of experienced venture capitalists with more than 35 technology executives and experts (CTOs, CIOs, Chief Scientists, Principal Engineers, Professors at Stanford and Berkeley) with significant tenures at top 100 technology companies and research institutions worldwide.

DCVC focuses on seed, Series A, and growth stage companies that apply deep compute, big data and IT infrastructure technologies to transform giant industries.

Creating value to make the world a better place

DCVC backs entrepreneurs solving trillion-dollar problems to multiply the benefits of capitalism for everyone while reducing its costs.

Its investments have created tens of billions of dollars of value while making the world a markedly better place–from rockets to robots, from molecular manufacturing™ to microbes, and from applied AI to autonomous systems. DCVC partners with the most innovative scientific and business minds applying Deep Tech to create the next great industrial giants.
