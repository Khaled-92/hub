Digital First Card Programs

Nymcard empowers people with better payment tools and provides a realistic alternative to cash by providing a modern card issuing and processing platform for financial institutions, banks and FinTechs. The company digitize the payment card experience end to end, helping cut down program costs and launch innovative programs in as little as 8 weeeks.

 

Modern | Agile | Customizable

Nymcard explores innovation in issuance and payments, ensuring its platform is modern with a user centric design and open API architecture to build the perfect experience without the complexity of legacy issuer processers; agile with the possibility to deploy new card programs and payment solutions in weeks instead of months or years; and customizable with consumers able to launch virtual, plastic and tokenized payment cards with flexibility for single of multi-use. The platform is highly customizable to support any current or future payment experience.

 

The Bridge to Digital Payments

NymCard is a payments platform built on three fundamental pillars: User Experience, Security, Data. Nymcard’s teams combine the right mix of modern mobile product competencies, country specific experiences and regional partnerships, working in a flexibile and locally relevant approach in each country by partnering with local financial institutions and providing operational support while leveraging a common product for agility and economies of sale.
