Who we are
TruKKer is a digital freight solutions platform that moves hundreds of homes across GCC and North Africa

TruKKer is MENA's largest digital freight solutions platform that operates thousands of trucks across all major lanes and transactions.

Users can book a truck in less than 60 seconds with instant pricing and guaranteed availability. TruKKer offers rates for a single trip, long-term hire, and contract logistics.

 

 Providing and operating multiple truck types for multiple end markets

TruKKer’s real time data and analytics power its predictive algorithms to determine supply information in real time and up to 48 hours in the future to allow immediate demand fulfilment and advance bookings.

Its extensive network of trucks operates all over the GCC and North Africa, and it is a single provider of multiple truck types from 1 Ton pick-ups to 50 Ton trailers.

 

TruKKer is on a mission to optimize logistics and fleet management using cutting-edge technology

TruKKer services 150+ businesses every day and 500+ retail clients daily for their cargo movement requirements including line haul, port movements, home relocations and last mile. Its operations are spread across 16 offices, including 12 ports across the Arab peninsula and supports Last-Mile Delivery (all over UAE), containerized movements from ports, domestic cargo distribution and cross-border road freight including customs clearance at the GCC borders.

TruKKer is dedicated to continuous innovation and keeping ahead of the competition from traditional players.
