Who we are
Enabling users to book, organize and pay for sports and social events via a registered network

Krews is a UAE-born social networking platform where users can book, organize, and pay for sports and social events with a network of gyms, personal trainers, sports venues and governing bodies.

Whether it’s looking for a fun chill out session or wanting to partner with buddies to play a game of football, Krews is the platform that will get you the company you need.

Interactive | Social | Vibrant

Krews is a community-based Apple and Android app helping users to live life to the fullest by eliminating the hassle of organizing sporting and social events in the vibrant UAE.

 

A tech-enabled social networking platform for sports and social events 

The Krews team have developed a tech-enabled social networking platform where users can book, organize and pay for sports and social events.

The social solution allows users to plan and organize events at the tap of a button - no phone calls, no messages and certainly no more emails to sort out plans.
