Who we are
Sarwa's platform makes expert investing easy, secure and affordable

Sarwa is an online financial advisor that takes the hurdles out of investing, while helping its users maximise returns at a risk they are comfortable with.

The platform aims to offer its customers the simplest and most cost-effective way to invest their earnings for long-term financial growth.

Combining proven investment strategies with technology that drives down costs

Sarwa helps people invest in fully-diversified portfolios of low-cost index funds, based on their risk profile. It uses cutting-edge technology to simplify the entire investing experience and provide the best possible return.

Sarwa does this by offering easy rebalancing, dividend reinvesting, and tax optimization - services that were very time consuming and complicated to do individually or were only available to the ultra rich.

 

Sarwa is the first hybrid automated investment platform in the region

Sarwa is backed by a team of world-class financial experts and the best technology talent. The company works hard to implement time-tested investment strategies and develop an investment experience that’s personalized.

Its talented team includes experts who bring decades of investments and technology experience to Sarwa. They've worked at companies such as Merrill Lynch, UBS, Accenture, Goldman Sachs and have chaired the boards of banks such as J.P. Morgan Chase Switzerland.
