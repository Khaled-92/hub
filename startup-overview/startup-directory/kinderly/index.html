Who we are
Providing resources, tools and digital solutions to help childcare professionals be at their best for children

Kinderly works with expert partners, including Abu Dhabi Early Childhood Authority, Abu Dhabi Department of Education and Knowledge, Ministry of Education, Bath Spa University and early childhood professionals to build and deliver best in-class software and complete quality enhancement solutions for early childhood educators, professionals and establishments.

Giving Children the Best Possible Start

Kinderly provides educators a secure and compliant cloud-based platform to easily capture children’s learning and developmental progress, view developmental progress of all children at a glance, encourage positive engagement with parents through a parent portal, and easily manage accounting and invoicing.

 

Award-winning Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) software supporting early years settings and professionals to be outstanding.

Kinderly is passionate about ensuring all children have a great start in life. Working in collaboration with early years settings and experts, Kinderly makes it quick and easy for the childcare setting to capture a child’s EYFS development milestones and share these incredible moments along with daily activities with parents.

With resources and tools at the click of a button and at their fingertips, childcare practioners can spend more time with children and parents are more engaged with their learning.

Founder & CEO - Kinderly

Geraint Barton

As a father of two young boys, Geraint founded Kinderly based on his first-hand experience and his personal desire to be fully engaged in his children’s early years learning and development journey. Prior to Kinderly, Geraint spent 11 years as big biological data analyst & software developer at Imperial College and established LabBook, the world’s first e-lab book on Android. His positive, common sense approach, calm under stress and sense of humour makes Kinderly a great place to work.

“The Hub71 team and program have been invaluable in helping Kinderly become established in the UAE, with their generous Incentive Program allowing us to recruit the best employees and quickly grow the team in Abu Dhabi.”
