Who we are
dopay is building financial solutions for tomorrow's economies by offering digital payroll you can trust

dopay is committed to transforming the lives of both employers and employees.

It automates the payroll process for employers and provides a debit card and mobile app for unbanked employees, meaning that employers no longer have to handle large amounts of cash and includes employees in the world of electronic payments and other financial services.

Simple | Secure | Fast

dopay makes paying employees and contractors simple – allowing users to spend time growing their business and not thinking about payroll.

Employers can pay one or multiple employees, schedule payments and open a dopay account for each employee, instantly, using the dopay for business app.

 

dopay is Democratizing Finance

dopay is building financial solutions for tomorrow's economies. The company has offices in London, Cairo and Ghana and its investors include the investors behind Uber, Twilio and Transferwise, as well as leading bankers from around the world.
