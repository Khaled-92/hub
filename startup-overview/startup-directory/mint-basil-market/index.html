Who we are
Mint Basil Market makes healthy living easy and accessible to all.

Mint Basil Market is an online destination for curated and trusted healthy natural products from food, natural beauty and cosmetics to eco-friendly household cleaning products.

The Mint Basil Market team also provide unique content from its community of health experts, providing users with health tips, recipes and product recommendations.

Easy | Healthy | Efficient

Mint Basil Market delivers healthy natural products from food, skincare and cosmetics and eco-friendly household cleaning products, all through its dedicated e-commerce platform.

With free delivery all over Lebanon, the experts at Mint Basil Market have created an easy and efficient offering for users to seeking to follow a healthier lifestyle.

 

 

Delivering a variety of healthy products to your door

Mint Basil Market is an e-commerce platform that provides healthy products at more affordable prices ranging from organic, gluten-free, vegan food products and all-natural cosmetics and cleaning products delivered to your door.

The website was designed to make it easier for health-conscious customers to find their favorite brands and discover new ones.
