Life in Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi has quickly risen to become a global hub of innovation with a clear vision to shape the future, a place where growth ambitions can materialize.

Internationally Diverse

The capital city of a nation with more than 200 nationalities, Abu Dhabi is an international city where founders, entrepreneurs and investors from around the world can feel at home.

 

Globally connected

Abu Dhabi is a globally connected city at the crossroads of East and West located no more than an 8-hour flight from 80% of the world’s population.

 

 

Knowledge and Cultural Hub

Consistently ranked among the world’s safest cities, and home to world renowned education and cultural institutions, Abu Dhabi is an ideal place to live and raise a family.

 

 

Safest City in the World

For three consecutive years, Abu Dhabi has been awarded for its extremely high safety, based on data from Numbeo covering more than 70,000 people.
